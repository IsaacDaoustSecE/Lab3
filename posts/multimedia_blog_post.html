Digital Album Collection Creating Wordclouds
Digital Album Collection Isaac • Sep 19, 2024

Discollect is a website where you can collect albums by listening to them on Spotify. These albums will then show on your public profile for others to see. Whether you're into classic rock, hip-hop, jazz, or electronic, Discollect lets you build a personal album collection that reflects your unique taste in music.

What makes this platform stand out is its integration with Spotify—there's no need to manually add albums. Simply listen, and Discollect will track your play history, automatically adding albums to your collection as soon as you’ve given them a proper listen. It’s more than just a music library; it’s a reflection of your listening journey.

By curating your collection, you not only show off the music you love but also discover what others are listening to. Your public profile lets friends and followers explore your tastes, find new albums, and even see overlaps between their collections and yours. Whether you're into uncovering hidden gems or keeping up with the latest releases, Discollect makes it easy to track your listening habits and share them with others. Discollect is perfect for anyone who loves music and wants to visualize their collection in a dynamic way.
